    X3ML Mapping¶

    This section is dedicated to share the X3ML mapping used by the CORDH consortium for transforming their XML data to RDF. Models were developed using 3M and computed used X3ML, both tools developed by FORTH ICS.

    Each institutions will present and describe their mappings, together with their URI generator policies as well as, when available, their data (or a link to them).

    I Tatti¶

    Getty Project¶

    X3ML Mapping definition and related Generator policy used in the Getty Project by I Tatti. The project involved the mapping of information related to Artists, Institutions, Artworks, Collections and photos.
