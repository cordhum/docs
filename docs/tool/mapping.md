# X3ML Mapping

This section is dedicated to share the [X3ML](https://github.com/isl/x3ml) mapping used by the CORDH consortium for transforming their XML data to RDF.  Models were developed using [3M](https://github.com/ncarboni/ESU2018_CRM_Workshop/blob/master/3M) and computed used [X3ML](https://github.com/isl/x3ml), both tools developed by [FORTH ICS](http://www.ics.forth.gr/isl/).

Each institutions will present and describe their mappings, together with their URI generator policies as well as, when available, their data (or a link to them).


## I Tatti

### FotoIndex Project

X3ML Mapping definition and related Generator policy used in the [FotoIndex Project](#) by [I Tatti](http://itatti.harvard.edu). The project involved the mapping of information related to Artists, Institutions, Artworks, Collections and photos.
